Participate

Thank you for your interest in participating in the work of Lawrance's Destination. Your support is crucial in helping us empower individuals and communities through education and skill development. There are several ways you can get involved:

Donate:

  • One-Time Donation: Make a one-time donation to support our programs and initiatives.
  • Monthly Giving: Become a monthly donor and provide ongoing support to our cause.

Volunteer:

  • Tutoring/Mentoring: Volunteer your time to tutor or mentor individuals in our programs.
  • Event Support: Help us organize and run fundraising events and community outreach programs.

Corporate Partnership:

  • Sponsorship: Partner with us through sponsorship opportunities for our events and programs.
  • Employee Engagement: Engage your employees in volunteer activities or fundraising efforts to support our cause.

Goods Donation:

  • School Supplies: Donate school supplies, books, and educational materials for our beneficiaries.
  • Clothing and Essentials: Donate clothing, hygiene products, and other essentials for those in need.

Attend Events:

  • Fundraising Events: Join us at our fundraising events to show your support and help raise funds for our programs.

Spread the Word:

  • Follow us on social media and share our posts to help raise awareness about our work.
  • Encourage your friends, family, and colleagues to support Lawrance's Destination.

We are grateful for the support from organizations like Investec and Eskom, as well as the teachers from Ndlela High School, healthcare providers, and social workers who have offered their support. Your participation, whether through donations, volunteering, or attending events, makes a significant impact on the lives of those we serve. Together, we can make a difference. Thank you for being a part of Lawrance's Destination.
